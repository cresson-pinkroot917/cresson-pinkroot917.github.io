📝 About the project

This software allows you to record video from your webcam, audio from your microphone, and your computer screen. You can also take snapshots or photos. It runs inside your web browser. You host the files on your own server. This gives you full control over your media files.

💻 System requirements

Your computer must meet these basic needs:

  • Windows 10 or Windows 11 operating system.
  • A modern web browser like Chrome, Edge, or Firefox.
  • A working webcam connected to your device.
  • A working microphone for audio input.
  • An active internet connection for the initial setup.

🚀 Getting started

Follow these steps to set up the recorder on your Windows machine:

  1. Visit the official page to download the project files.
  2. Click the green Code button and select Download ZIP.
  3. Find the downloaded file in your Downloads folder.
  4. Right-click the file and select Extract All.
  5. Choose a folder where you want to keep the application.

⚙️ How to use the recorder

Once you extract the folder, open the index file in your web browser.

Recording Video and Audio:

  1. Grant permission for the browser to use your camera and microphone when prompted.
  2. Select your device from the settings menu.
  3. Press the Record button to begin your session.
  4. Press the Stop button to end the capture.
  5. Download the resulting file to your computer.

Capturing Your Screen:

  1. Open the screen capture tab in the interface.
  2. Choose whether to record your entire screen or a specific browser window.
  3. Click Share to confirm the recording area.
  4. The system saves your recording automatically when you stop the stream.

🛡️ Privacy and storage

Because this tool is self-hosted, your data stays under your control. The media files do not travel through third-party servers. Your browser processes the video and audio streams locally. You choose exactly where to save your final files on your hard drive. This setup protects your privacy and keeps your content secure.

🔧 Troubleshooting common issues

If the application does not detect your webcam, check your Windows privacy settings. Ensure that your browser has permission to access your hardware devices. Refresh your browser page if the preview window does not show video immediately. Make sure no other apps are using your webcam at the same time.
